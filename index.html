about me


Hello my name is Andrea Ochoa. I was born in San Francisco and raised in Oakland. Being a designer and artists means I can bring to life ideas that only exists in my imagination. Some things that I'm passionate about is matcha and wellness which leads me to my top fives page. Matcha is made from green tea leaves and has historically symbolized tranquility, health, and longivity. I wanted my website to be a space that bring that peacefulness and in my unordered lists I share some of my favorite self-care practices that has improved my physical and mental health tremendously.
