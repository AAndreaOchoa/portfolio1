# project overview
The major choices/changes I made when revising my site is adding a new "Works" page to showcase a design project I worked on this semester, converted pixels to percentages and changed most font sizes to "em", and when the screensize is 600px the navigation moves to the bottom of the page but the logo and email button stay at the top of the page. I changed the location of the navigation becuase i noticed that most mobile pages only had a hamburger menu to display their navighation to other pages, but on social media apps the have navigation buttons in the bottom. This design decision kept my website visually balanced because if i hade the naviation stacked on either side of the screen it would look off-putting. The most challenging part of this project is make the buttons, logo, and mail gif responsive but throughout this semester my patience with coding has grew and it brings me such satisfaction when I bring what I am thing visually to life.
